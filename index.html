Core Loop

WHAT THE PLAYER ACTUALLY DOES

The best publisher pitch is a loop, not a lore summary. Players explore, identify the obstacle, use the right animal ability or switch roles, trigger a satisfying or silly outcome, and push into the next challenge.

Explore compact spaces
Readable 2D spaces let players quickly spot routes, hazards, and interaction opportunities.
Swap or cooperate
Solo players alternate roles while co-op players divide tasks naturally, creating two valid play fantasies from one design spine.
Solve by personality
Each character is functionally different enough that choosing who acts becomes part of the fun.

Who is this for?

THE RIGHT GAME FOR THE RIGHT PLAYERS

Target players
  • Co-op players who like laughing through puzzle platformers with friends.
  • Players who enjoy clear role division and asymmetric cooperation.
  • Streamers and creators looking for short, high-reaction indie games.
Why this game fits them
  • Solo players tag between roles themselves — it feels co-op without needing a second player.
  • Co-op players genuinely split roles: one opens the path, the other gets through.
  • Streamers get instantly readable reactions, short sessions, and natural comedy from mismatched roles.
Story

THE STORY SUPPORTS THE PLAY

When the Pig King's minions raid a peaceful squirrel village and steal its precious acorns, DA-munk and GO-rani set out to take them back. Simple, legible, emotionally clean — it gives players a reason to move without burying the game under lore.
